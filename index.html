About the Galactic Empire

The Galactic Empire are a union that wants to destroy the Jedi. They are the main antagonists in the Star Wars universe. Their leader, Darth Sidious, plans to take over every planet and take over the galaxy. In Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith, Darth Sidious tricks Anakin Skywalker, a very skilled Jedi, into joining the Empire. Soon, Anakin Skywalker became Darth Vader, a powerful Sith Lord. In Return of the Jedi (episode 6) the Empire were defeated by the Rebels and were forced to surrender.
